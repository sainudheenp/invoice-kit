Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Is invoicekit really free?
Yes, completely free. There are no paid plans, no subscriptions, no premium features. No credit card needed. It's an open-source project.
Does it require internet?
No. The app works fully offline. You only need internet for the first page load to download the app files. After that, everything runs locally in your browser.
Where is my data stored?
All your data — companies, invoices, receipts, logos — is stored in your browser's IndexedDB database on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. You can export a backup JSON file at any time.
Which currencies are supported?
10 presets: Omani Rial (OMR), US Dollar (USD), Euro (EUR), British Pound (GBP), Saudi Riyal (SAR), UAE Dirham (AED), Kuwaiti Dinar (KWD), Qatari Riyal (QAR), Bahraini Dinar (BHD), Egyptian Pound (EGP). You can also configure a completely custom currency.
Can I add my company logo to invoices?
Yes. You can upload a logo image, paste SVG code, and customize your brand colors. You can also upload a company seal and signature for the PDF output.
Does it support VAT / tax calculation?
Yes. Each company has a configurable VAT percentage and registration number. VAT is automatically calculated on invoices. Discounts are also supported.
Can I edit or delete saved documents?
Yes. Every saved invoice and receipt can be edited (all fields restored), reprinted, downloaded as PDF, exported as text, or permanently deleted from the Documents page.
Is there Arabic language support?
Yes. All documents include English and Arabic side by side. Company names, subtitles, and document labels are fully bilingual with proper right-to-left rendering.
